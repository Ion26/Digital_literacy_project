# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone  
- Do not accept unknown payment requests  
- Always verify the sender before making transactions  
- Use trusted and official payment apps only  
- Enable app lock and two-factor authentication  
- Regularly check bank statements and transaction history  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Report fraud immediately on https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud
