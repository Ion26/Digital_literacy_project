# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional profile
- Think before posting content
- Respect others online
- Use privacy settings wisely
- Share informative and positive content

## ❌ Don’ts
- Avoid sharing personal/sensitive information
- Do not engage in online arguments
- Avoid posting inappropriate content
- Do not spread misinformation
- Avoid cyberbullying or harassment
